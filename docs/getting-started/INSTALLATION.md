# Bridge Companion - Installation Guide

This guide will help you install Bridge Companion as an app on your phone so you can access it offline during your cultural learning experience in Bahrain.

## Why Install as an App?

- **Works Offline**: Access all content without internet connection
- **Home Screen Icon**: Launch like any other app
- **Faster Loading**: Content is cached on your device
- **Full Screen**: No browser bars taking up space

---

## Installation Instructions

Choose your device type below:

### 📱 iPhone / iPad (Safari)

1. **Open Safari** (must use Safari, not Chrome or other browsers)
   
2. **Visit the app**: Go to the URL provided by your team leader
   
3. **Tap the Share button** 
   - Look for the square with an arrow pointing up at the bottom of the screen
   - ![Share Icon](https://via.placeholder.com/30x30/007AFF/FFFFFF?text=↑)

4. **Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"**
   - You may need to scroll down in the menu
   - Look for a plus (+) icon

5. **Name the app** (optional)
   - The default name "Bridge Companion" will appear
   - Tap "Add" in the top right

6. **Find the app**
   - Look for the Bridge Companion icon on your home screen
   - It will have a custom icon (not a browser icon)

7. **Open and use**
   - Tap the icon to launch
   - On first launch, it will download all content for offline use
   - Wait for "Ready for offline use" message

**Troubleshooting iPhone**:
- If you don't see "Add to Home Screen", make sure you're using Safari, not Chrome
- If the app shows a browser bar, you opened it in Safari instead of from the home screen icon
- Delete and reinstall if content doesn't load properly

---

### 🤖 Android (Chrome)

1. **Open Chrome** (recommended browser for Android)

2. **Visit the app**: Go to the URL provided by your team leader

3. **Tap the menu** (three dots in top right)

4. **Select "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"**
   - Newer Android versions will show "Install app"
   - Older versions may show "Add to Home screen"

5. **Confirm installation**
   - Tap "Install" when prompted
   - The app will be added to your home screen and app drawer

6. **Open the app**
   - Find Bridge Companion in your app drawer or home screen
   - On first launch, content will download for offline access
   - Wait for "Ready for offline use" confirmation

**Troubleshooting Android**:
- If you don't see "Install app", try "Add to Home screen" instead
- Some Android devices call it "Add to desktop"
- If offline mode isn't working, check that Chrome has storage permission

---

### 💻 Desktop / Laptop (Optional)

#### Chrome / Edge

1. **Open Chrome or Microsoft Edge**

2. **Visit the app URL**

3. **Click the install icon** in the address bar
   - Look for a ⊕ or 💻 icon near the right side of the address bar
   - OR click the three-dot menu → "Install Bridge Companion"

4. **Click "Install"** when prompted

5. **Use the app**
   - The app will open in its own window
   - Access from your applications folder or desktop shortcut
   - Works offline after initial download

#### Safari (Mac)

Safari on Mac doesn't support installing web apps the same way. Instead:

1. **Use as a bookmark**: Add to bookmarks bar for quick access
2. **OR use Chrome/Edge**: Better PWA support on desktop

## Using the App Offline

### Before Your Trip

1. **Install the app** using instructions above
2. **Open the app** and let it fully load
3. **Browse through all tabs** to ensure content is cached
4. **Test offline**: 
   - Turn on airplane mode
   - Open the app
   - Verify all content is accessible

### During Your Trip

- **The app works completely offline** - no internet needed for core content
- **External links** (YouTube videos, article links) require internet
  - These will show as "Requires internet" when offline
  - Save them for when you have WiFi
- **Scripture reference links** work when internet is available

### If Content Doesn't Update

The app caches content for offline use. If your team leader updates the content:

1. **Connect to WiFi**
2. **Close the app completely** (swipe up to close, don't just minimize)
3. **Reopen the app**
4. **It will check for updates** and download new content automatically
5. **Look for "Content updated"** notification

## Uninstalling the App

### iPhone

1. **Long press the app icon** on your home screen
2. **Tap "Remove App"**
3. **Select "Delete App"**

### Android

1. **Long press the app icon**
2. **Drag to "Uninstall"** or tap the info icon and select "Uninstall"

### Desktop

- **Chrome/Edge**: Right-click the app icon → Uninstall
- **Mac**: Remove from Applications folder

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to reinstall before each trip?**  
A: No. Once installed, the app stays on your device. Content can be updated without reinstalling.

**Q: Can I install on multiple devices?**  
A: Yes! Install on your phone, tablet, and backup device.

**Q: Will this use my data plan?**  
A: Only during initial installation and content updates. All offline use is data-free.

**Q: What if I can't install (company phone restrictions)?**  
A: You can still use it as a regular website in your browser, but offline features won't work.

**Q: Can I share this with local believers?**  
A: Check with your team leader first regarding content distribution policies.

**Q: The app looks different than described?**  
A: Make sure you're opening the installed app icon, not the website in a browser.
